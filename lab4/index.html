Making Strawberry Rhubarb Pie:

1. Gather Ingredients 2. Crust Preparation 2.1. Blend flour, sugar, shortening, butter and salt in pastry blender/ 2.2. Mix egg, vinegar and water/ 2.3. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients/ 2.4. Toss dough with flour, and chill/ 2.5. Divide dough in two discs and place one in the pie dish/ 3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F 4. Filling Preparation 4.1. Mix rhubard, strawberries, tapioca, flour, lemon zest, lemon juice, cinnamon and vanilla/ 4.2. Pour mixture into chilled crust/ 4.3. Dot butter on the top of the pie/ 4.4. Brush edges with egg whites/ 4.5. Roll out second dough and place over filling/ 4.6. Crimp the edges/ 4.7. Brush with egg whites and granulated sugar/ 4.8. Put foil around the collar of the pie/ 5. Bake 5.1. Bake at 425F for 15 minutes/ 5.2. Decrease temperature to 375F/ 5.3. Bake for 45-50 minutes/ 5.4. Take pie out of the oven/ 6. Let the pie cool before serving.
