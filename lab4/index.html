Making Strawberry Rhubarb Pie:

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Crust Preparation
    1. Blend flour, sugar, shortening, butter and salt in pastry blender/
    2. Mix egg, vinegar and water/
    3. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients/
    4. Toss dough with flour, and chill/
    5. Divide dough in two discs and place one in the pie dish/
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  4. Filling Preparation
    1. Mix rhubard, strawberries, tapioca, flour, lemon zest, lemon juice, cinnamon and vanilla/
    2. Pour mixture into chilled crust/
    3. Dot butter on the top of the pie/
    4. Brush edges with egg whites/
    5. Roll out second dough and place over filling/
    6. Crimp the edges/
    7. Brush with egg whites and granulated sugar/
    8. Put foil around the collar of the pie/
  5. Bake
    1. Bake at 425F for 15 minutes/
    2. Decrease temperature to 375F/
    3. Bake for 45-50 minutes/
    4. Take pie out of the oven/
  6. Let the pie cool before serving.
